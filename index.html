About Trivia Quiz...

Welcome to Trivia Quiz game.
Are you ready to test yourself answering challenging questions on your favourite category?
Choose a category and a difficulty level and then you can start the game.
The questions will be True/False or Multiple Choice, and the program will select them for you randomly...
10 Questions for the easy level
13 Questions for the medium level
15 Questions for the hard level
At the end of the game the program will judge you performance...
You can continue the game choosing the continue button, which will keep your score while you are starting a new round of questions, or you can reset the count starting a new session clicking the home button.

Good Luck and enjoy.
