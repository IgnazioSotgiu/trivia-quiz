About Trivia Quiz...

Welcome to Trivia Quiz game. Are you ready to test yourself answering challenging questions on your favourite category? First choose which category and then the difficulty level. Then you can start the game. The questions can be True/False or Multiple Choice, and the program will select them for you randomly... 10 Questions for the easy level, 13 Questions for the medium level, 15 Questions for the hard level... At the end of the game the program will judge you performance.... Good Luck and enjoy. Trivia Quiz Team.
